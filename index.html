Ongoing Research

Enhancing Diabetic Prediction Through Machine Learning Pipeline and Rigorous Validation

Our ongoing research focuses on leveraging machine learning (ML) models to predict diabetes onset. Employing a pipeline approach, we aim to streamline the process of data preprocessing, feature selection, and model training to enhance prediction accuracy. A key emphasis lies in rigorous validation techniques to rigorously test and refine our model's performance. By integrating diverse data sources and employing advanced ML algorithms, we seek to develop a robust predictive model capable of identifying individuals at risk of developing diabetes. Our research endeavors to contribute to early detection and proactive management of diabetes, ultimately improving healthcare outcomes and quality of life for affected individuals.
